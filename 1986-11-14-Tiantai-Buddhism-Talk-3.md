Those 75 or 100 kinds of dharma means the analysis of the phenomenal world. So the phenomenal world consists of 75 dharmas or 100 kinds of dharmas. Those dharmas are the essential factors of existence, and create something. And also they are interconnected to each other, they penetrate each other. So what makes them possible to create something?
